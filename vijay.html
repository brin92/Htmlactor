VIJAY

Vijay, whose full name is Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, is a leading actor in Tamil cinema. He was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He is the son of filmmaker S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. Vijay began his acting career as a child artist and debuted as a lead in the 1992 film Naalaiya Theerpu. He rose to fame with hits like Ghilli, Thuppakki, Mersal, and Master. Known for his mass appeal, he is often called “Thalapathy” by fans, which means "commander" in Tamil. Vijay is admired for his dance skills, charismatic screen presence, and versatility. Apart from films, he is known for his philanthropic work and quiet donations to social causes. He has a huge fan base not just in Tamil Nadu but across India and overseas. As of 2025, Vijay is also stepping into politics with growing public attention on his future moves.
